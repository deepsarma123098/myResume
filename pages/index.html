About Me

I graduated with a degree in Instrumentation Engineering from Assam Engineering College. My career began at Assam Bio Refinery, where I served as an Executive in the Instrumentation department for 1 year and 7 months. During this time, I honed my skills in instrumentation and gained valuable experience in the bio-refinery sector. After that, I transitioned to Pride East Entertainment Pvt. Ltd., taking on the role of Project Coordinator. Over the course of 5 months, I managed various projects, further expanding my expertise in project management and coordination. Driven by a passion for technology and a desire for new challenges, I decided to switch to the IT field. I am now focused on building a career in tech, leveraging my engineering background and newly acquired skills in software development.

Video Streaming App

VidStream is a video-sharing platform backend developed using Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. The application allows users to upload, manage, and stream video content, with Cloudinary integrated as a cloud service for efficient media storage and delivery. It features a robust user authentication system with JWT access tokens and refresh tokens for secure session management.

Mega Blog App

The blog project is built using React The application allows users to create, view, edit, and delete blog posts seamlessly. Redux is integrated into the project to manage the application's state efficiently, ensuring that data flow between components is predictable and centralized. For the backend, I've utilized Appwrite, an open-source backend-as-a-service (BaaS) platform that offers a variety of tools and services.
